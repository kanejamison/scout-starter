This website follows the following guidelines and policies to protect our unit members' privacy as well as to maintain the integrity of our unit and Scouting as a whole.

  • Safety is paramount within Scouting. We do not mention individual Scouts by name. The site will consider the safety and privacy of unit members and participants by obtaining the necessary permissions to release information about or images of any individual.
  • The content of the site will be appropriate for Scouting.
  • The site will not add links to any sites that may contain material deemed inappropriate for Scouting.
  • The site will not add any advertisements or commercial endorsements.
  • The site will not engage in the sale of Scouting merchandise.
  • The site will not engage in direct fundraising or donation activities.
  • The site will not replicate any BSA publications currently for sale.
  • The site will abide by all laws regarding copyrights, trademarks, and other intellectual property, and by those pertaining to the internet.
