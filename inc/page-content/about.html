About Our Pack — Cub Scouts

We are a Cub Scout pack serving youth in our community. Cub Scouting is a year-round program for boys and girls in kindergarten through fifth grade that builds character, citizenship, and personal fitness through fun, family-centered activities.

Our pack meets regularly throughout the school year and welcomes scouts of all experience levels. Whether your child is brand new to scouting or transferring from another pack, we'd love to have them join our family.

What We Do

  • Monthly pack meetings and weekly den meetings
  • Pinewood Derby
  • Camping and outdoor adventures
  • Community service projects
  • Raingutter Regatta
  • Scout Sunday and flag ceremonies

Our Leadership

Our pack is run entirely by volunteer parents and community members passionate about giving kids a great scouting experience. Interested in volunteering? Reach out — we'll find a role that fits your schedule.

About Our Troop — Scouts BSA

We are a Scouts BSA troop serving youth in our community. Scouts BSA is a year-round program for young men and women ages 11–17 that develops leadership, self-reliance, and citizenship through outdoor adventure and community service.

Our troop uses the patrol method — small groups of scouts who plan and lead their own activities with adult support. We welcome scouts transferring from a Cub Scout pack or joining scouting for the first time.

What We Do

  • Weekly troop meetings and patrol activities
  • Monthly campouts and high-adventure trips
  • Merit badge work and rank advancement toward Eagle Scout
  • Community service and Eagle Scout projects
  • Courts of Honor celebrating scout achievements
  • Jamboree and council events

Our Leadership

Our troop is led by experienced Scoutmasters and volunteer adult leaders dedicated to helping every scout reach their potential. Interested in volunteering? Reach out — we'd love your help.
