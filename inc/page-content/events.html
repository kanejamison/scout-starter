Pack Events — Cub Scouts

We have a full calendar of activities throughout the scouting year. Below are some annual highlights — check back often as new events are added.

  • Pinewood Derby — Scouts design, build, and race their own wooden cars.
  • Raingutter Regatta — Scouts build and race small sailboats.
  • Fall Campout — An overnight adventure for scouts and their families.
  • Blue & Gold Banquet — Our annual celebration dinner honoring scouts and their achievements.
  • Scouting for Food — Community food drive benefiting local food banks.
  • Arrow of Light Crossover — Ceremony celebrating Webelos scouts as they bridge to Scouts BSA.

Regular Meetings

Den meetings are held weekly or biweekly. Pack-wide meetings are held once a month. Contact us for the current meeting schedule and location.

Troop Events — Scouts BSA

We have a full calendar of activities throughout the scouting year. Below are some annual highlights — check back often as new events are added.

  • Courts of Honor — Quarterly ceremonies recognizing rank advancements and merit badge achievements.
  • Monthly Campouts — Scouts plan and lead their own overnight trips throughout the year.
  • Summer Camp — A week-long Scouting America camp where scouts earn merit badges and enjoy high-adventure activities.
  • Eagle Scout Boards of Review — Celebrating scouts who achieve scouting's highest rank.
  • Scouting for Food — Community food drive benefiting local food banks.
  • High Adventure — Older scouts may participate in treks to Philmont, Sea Base, or Northern Tier.

Regular Meetings

Troop meetings are held weekly. Patrols may meet separately between troop meetings. Contact us for the current meeting schedule and location.
