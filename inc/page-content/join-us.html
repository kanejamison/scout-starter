Join Our Pack — Cub Scouts

Cub Scouting is one of the best things you can do for your child. Scouts develop confidence, make lifelong friends, learn real skills, and have a ton of fun along the way. We'd love to have your family join us.

Who Can Join?

Cub Scouts is open to boys and girls in kindergarten through fifth grade (ages 5–10). No prior scouting experience required — we welcome everyone.

How to Join

  1. Come to a meeting — Attend a pack or den meeting as a guest. See what we're about and meet other families.
  2. Register online — Visit scouting.org/join to register and pay dues through BSA's national system.
  3. Get your uniform — We'll help you figure out what you need. The Scout Shop and many online retailers carry everything.
  4. Show up and have fun — That's really all there is to it.

Not sure if scouting is right for your family? Send us a message — we're happy to answer any questions.

Join Our Troop — Scouts BSA

Scouts BSA offers one of the most rewarding youth experiences available. Scouts build real leadership skills, earn merit badges, go on incredible adventures, and work toward the Eagle Scout rank — a distinction recognized for life. We'd love to have you join our troop.

Who Can Join?

Scouts BSA is open to young men and women ages 11–17 (or who have completed fifth grade and earned the Arrow of Light). No prior scouting experience required.

How to Join

  1. Come to a meeting — Attend a troop meeting as a guest. Meet our scouts and leaders and see the patrol method in action.
  2. Register online — Visit scouting.org/join to register and pay dues through BSA's national system.
  3. Get your uniform — We'll point you in the right direction. The Scout Shop and online retailers carry everything you need.
  4. Join a patrol and get started — You'll be placed in a patrol of peers and begin working toward your first rank right away.

Not sure if scouting is right for you? Send us a message — we're happy to answer any questions.
