Who we are

Our unit is a registered Scouting unit with the Boy Scouts of America. This website is managed by unit leaders and volunteers.

Embedded content from other websites

Articles on this site may include embedded content (e.g. videos, images, articles, etc.). Embedded content from other websites behaves in the exact same way as if the visitor has visited the other website.

These websites may collect data about you, use cookies, embed additional third-party tracking, and monitor your interaction with that embedded content, including tracking your interaction with the embedded content if you have an account and are logged in to that website.

Who we share your data with

We may use a privacy-friendly analytics tool to track website sessions and pageviews. This may store information about your country and pages you visit, but does not store your IP address or other personally-identifying information.

We do not run any advertising or other tracking scripts on this website.

How long we retain your data

If you fill out our contact form or other forms on our website, your name, email address, and other message details are retained indefinitely.

For unit members or leaders that register as users on our website (if any), we also store the personal information they provide in their user profile. All users can see, edit, or delete their personal information at any time (except they cannot change their username). Website administrators can also see and edit that information.

What rights you have over your data

If you fill out a form on our website, you may request that we erase any personal data we hold about you. This does not include any data we are obliged to keep for administrative, legal, or security purposes.

Where your data is sent

Any form completions on this site are sent to our Committee Chair, Cubmaster, or another registered leader or volunteer within our unit designated to reply to inbound messages.

You may contact us for further information.
