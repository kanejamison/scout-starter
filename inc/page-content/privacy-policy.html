Pack 1234 is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information — such as your name and email address — when you contact us through this website or sign up for unit communications. We do not sell or share your information with third parties.

Cookies

This website may use cookies to improve your browsing experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

Children's Privacy

We follow Scouting America's youth protection guidelines. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. Photos of minors posted to this site do not include identifying information such as full names or school names.

Third-Party Services

This site may use third-party services such as Google Analytics or embedded maps. These services have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us.
